How the Integration Works:

  1. Form submission triggers Zoho CRM webform endpoint
  2. Data automatically maps to CRM lead fields (Name, Email, Phone, Notes)
  3. Lead tagged with "Intern" and "Onboarding" labels
  4. Workflow automation assigns lead to HR team
  5. Email notification sent to admin and welcome email to intern
